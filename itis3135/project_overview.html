Page Design Overview

1. Home

  • Purpose: Welcome users and promote key info
  • Audience: All visitors
  • Content: Banner, intro, featured events, nav links
  • JS: Slideshow of announcements

2. About Us

  • Purpose: Explain our mission and introduce leadership
  • Audience: Students, faculty, collaborators
  • Content: Officer bios, club history
  • JS: Toggle sections or tabs for bios

3. Events

  • Purpose: Display past/upcoming events
  • Audience: Members, newcomers
  • Content: Event list or grid with dates and photos
  • JS: Filter past/upcoming

4. Join Us

  • Purpose: Let users express interest and sign up
  • Audience: Prospective members
  • Content: Form with name, major, contact info
  • JS: Form validation

5. Gallery

  • Purpose: Highlight photos from past events
  • Audience: Public, interested students
  • Content: Interactive image gallery
  • JS: Image slideshow or lightbox

Dynamic Functionality & References

  • Slideshow: Used in Gallery to view multiple event photos
  • Form Validation: On Join Us page with JavaScript
  • Interactive Bios: Show/hide officer bios with toggle buttons
