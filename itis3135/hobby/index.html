When to Run?

The best time to run depends on your schedule and preferences. Many people enjoy running in the early morning or late evening to avoid the heat.

Here are some popular times to run and their benefits:

  • Morning: Cooler temperatures and energizes you for the day. However, it can be hard to wake up early.
  • Evening: Relieves stress and cooler temperatures. However, it can interfere with dinner or sleep.

How to Run?

If you're new to running, start slow and gradually increase your distance. Here are some tips:

  1. Wear proper running shoes.
  2. Warm up before running.
  3. Stay hydrated.
