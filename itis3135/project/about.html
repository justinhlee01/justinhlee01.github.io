About KASA

KASA is a student organization at UNC Charlotte focused on promoting Korean-American culture, identity, and community. We create inclusive spaces for cultural appreciation, education, and meaningful social interaction through events and initiatives on campus.

Our Mission

To foster unity through cultural celebration, provide leadership opportunities, and connect students with Korean heritage and values. KASA is committed to helping students feel a sense of belonging and cultural pride, whether they are Korean, Korean-American, or simply interested in Korean culture.

What We Do

We host cultural showcases, social mixers, collaborative events with other student orgs, and informational sessions. These events help spread awareness of Korean culture and bring the student body together through food, music, performances, and shared experiences.

Why It Matters

KASA supports cultural diversity on campus and helps students build lifelong friendships and networks. Our work enriches campus life and offers students an opportunity to engage in leadership, event planning, and cultural exploration.
