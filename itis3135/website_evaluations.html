Website Evaluations

Apple

Apple's website is a great example of clean, modern web design that follows CRAP principles effectively.

  • Contrast: Excellent use of bold typography and high contrast between text and background.
  • Repetition: Consistent design elements such as fonts, spacing, and color themes.
  • Alignment: Everything is perfectly aligned, creating a clean and professional look.
  • Proximity: Related content is grouped together logically, improving readability.

Ling's Cars

This website is chaotic and difficult to navigate, making it a great example of what NOT to do.

  • Contrast: Overuse of bright, clashing colors makes text hard to read.
  • Repetition: No consistency in fonts, images, or layout.
  • Alignment: Poor alignment makes the site look messy and unstructured.
  • Proximity: Elements are scattered randomly, making navigation confusing.
